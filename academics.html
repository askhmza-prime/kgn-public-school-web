Academics

Classes 1 to 8 | UP State Board | Hindi Medium

Our Curriculum

Classes 1 to 8

We follow the UP State Board curriculum with a focus on building strong foundational knowledge in all core subjects.

1️⃣

Class 1 & 2

Foundation stage — Hindi, English basics, Maths, EVS through activity-based learning.

3️⃣

Class 3 & 4

Building on basics — strengthening reading, writing, arithmetic and environmental science.

5️⃣

Class 5 & 6

Expanding knowledge — Hindi, Maths, Science, Social Science introduced at deeper level.

7️⃣

Class 7 & 8

Upper primary stage — preparing students for secondary education with strong conceptual clarity.

Subjects Offered

Core Subjects

📖

Hindi

Primary medium of instruction. Grammar, literature, writing skills and comprehension.

🔢

Mathematics

Arithmetic, geometry, and problem-solving skills from basic to advanced concepts.

🔬

Science / EVS

Environmental studies for lower classes, Science for upper primary — practical approach.

🌍

Social Science

History, Geography, Civics — building awareness about our world and society.

🔤

English

English language introduction — reading, writing, and basic communication skills.

🎨

Art & Drawing

Creative expression through drawing, painting, and art activities for all classes.

Our Approach

How We Teach

💬

Interactive Learning

Teachers encourage students to ask questions and participate actively in every class.

📋

Regular Assessment

Periodic tests, unit exams, and annual examinations to track student progress clearly.

👨‍👩‍👧

Parent Involvement

Regular parent-teacher meetings to discuss student progress and address any concerns.
