About K.G.N. Public School

Serving Moradabad with quality education since 2006

Our Story

Building Bright Futures Since 2006

K.G.N. Public School was established in 2006 with a noble vision — to provide quality, affordable education to the children of Moradabad. Located in the heart of Moradabad City, our school has been a pillar of primary education in this community.

As a private unaided institution under the Uttar Pradesh State Board, we follow the state-prescribed curriculum with Hindi as the primary medium of instruction — making quality education accessible to every family.

Over the years, we have nurtured hundreds of students, preparing them with a strong academic foundation and moral values that guide them throughout their lives.

Our Purpose

Vision, Mission & Values

🎯

Our Vision

To be a leading school in Moradabad that shapes responsible, educated, and morally strong citizens who contribute positively to society and the nation.

🚀

Our Mission

To provide affordable, quality education in Hindi medium to every child regardless of their background — building knowledge, character, and confidence.

💎

Our Values

Discipline, Respect, Honesty, Hard Work, and Compassion. We instil these values in every student from day one through education and example.
