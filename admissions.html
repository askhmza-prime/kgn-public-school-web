Admissions 2025–26

Join the K.G.N. family — Seats are limited!

🎓 Admissions are OPEN for Session 2025–26 | Classes 1 to 8 | Contact school for details

How to Apply

Admission Process

01

Visit School

Visit the school office during working hours and collect the admission enquiry form.

02

Submit Documents

Fill the form and submit all required documents at the school reception.

03

Verification

Documents are verified by the school administration. You will be notified of the status.

04

Fee Payment

Pay the school fee and collect the admission confirmation. Your child is now enrolled!

📋 Documents Required

  • ✓ Birth Certificate (Original + Photocopy)
  • ✓ Aadhar Card of Child & Parents
  • ✓ Previous Class Marksheet / Report Card
  • ✓ Transfer Certificate (TC) from previous school
  • ✓ 4 Passport Size Photographs
  • ✓ Residence Proof (Aadhar / Ration Card)
