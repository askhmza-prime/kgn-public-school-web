Important announcements and updates for students and parents

Admissions Open — Session 2025–26

Admissions are now open for Classes 1 to 8 for the upcoming academic session 2025–26. Parents are requested to contact the school office during working hours. Limited seats available.

📅 March 2025 · Admission
📝

Annual Examination Schedule — 2024–25

Annual examinations for all classes (1 to 8) will be conducted as per the schedule. Students are advised to start preparation well in advance. Admit cards will be distributed by class teachers.

📅 February 2025 · Exam

Summer Vacation Notice 2025

School will remain closed for summer vacations. The exact dates will be announced shortly. New session 2025–26 will begin in April. Parents are requested to note the dates.

📅 April 2025 · Holiday

Annual Sports Day Celebration

Annual Sports Day will be celebrated in the school playground. All students are encouraged to participate in various sports events. Parents are cordially invited to attend.

📅 January 2025 · Event

Public Holiday — Eid-ul-Fitr

School will remain closed on the occasion of Eid-ul-Fitr. Classes will resume on the next working day. Eid Mubarak to all students and their families.

📅 March 2025 · Holiday

Half-Yearly Exam Results Declared

Half-yearly examination results have been declared. Report cards will be distributed to students. Parents are requested to collect report cards from school office.

📅 December 2024 · Exam

Republic Day Celebration

Republic Day will be celebrated with great enthusiasm. Flag hoisting ceremony will be held in the school premises. All students and staff are requested to be present.

📅 January 2025 · Event

Documents Required for New Admission

Parents applying for new admissions must bring: Birth Certificate, Aadhar Card, Previous class marksheet/TC, 4 passport size photographs, and Residence proof.
